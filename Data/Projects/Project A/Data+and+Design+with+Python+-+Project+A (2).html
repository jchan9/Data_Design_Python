Most Popular Baby Names¶

This dataset can be downloaded HERE

The dataset displayed is of the most popular baby names in New York City. The data is based off of the baby's sex and the mother's ethnicity. There are multiple variables that are included. Here is a breakdown of them:

Year of Birth:

  • What year the baby is born. This dataset has information from years ranging from 2011-2014.

Gender:

  • Whether the baby is male or female can contribute largly to what name is being given.

Ethnicty:

  • This is NOT the ethnicity of the baby, but of the mother.

Child's First Name:

  • This is the name given to the child that is born.

Count:

  • This indicates how many children are named whatever is under Child's First Name, given that the following are the same: Year of birth, Gender, and Ethnicity(of mother).

Rank:

  • This indicates the popularity of the name given to the baby
